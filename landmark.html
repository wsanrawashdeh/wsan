🏰 Kerak Castle (قلعة الكرك)

🎥 Video Tour

📍 Location

Kerak Castle is located in Al-Karak city, southern Jordan. It stands on a hilltop about 950 meters above sea level.

View on Google Maps

🌟 Why I Chose This Landmark

I chose Kerak Castle because it represents the historical and cultural identity of Al-Karak. I visited it personally and was amazed by its ancient architecture and the view from the top. It made me feel proud of my heritage and inspired by the resilience of my city.
